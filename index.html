Eligibility Criteria 1: Recent graduate

This program is open to everyone who has graduated within the last two years. You can hold any degree from any branch as long as you are comfortable using computers and mobile phones. You are going to enjoy this course if prefer being practical and hands-on.

Eligibility Criteria 2: Quick learner

You should be comfortable with a fast pace. We are training you to succeed at your first job. You should try to learn and apply your learning as quickly as you can. We do not provide classroom training. Instead continually assign work to you and give you daily feedback. Our pace can seem fast for most freshers but it is expected in the industry.

Eligibility Criteria 3: Self Starter

Trainees who successfully complete this course are self-starters. They typically try things out on their own. They are used to Googling for solutions to their technical problems. Doing a project on your own interest is a good indicator that you will be able to complete the training program.

About Qxf2

We are experienced testers working at Qxf2. Qxf2 provides software testing services for startups. We produce a lot of open source material to help testers stay in touch with technical advancements in the field of testing. Qxf2 has been conducting a very successful internship program since 2016. Our interns have gone on to work at companies like Mphasis, Accenture, Temenos, Bosch, TCS, Qlik, StrikeTru, Surebot Technology Solutions, Infilect, Plaxonic Technologies and of course Qxf2. Over time, we realized that the way we helped our interns bridge the gap between college and the industry could be applicable to a wider range of engineers. The approach, techniques and tools used in this training program are a result of our learning from our previous internships and our own industry experience.
